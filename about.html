Pro-Duck-Tivity Timer

This project by Ducks in a Row aims to make everyday tasks fun and exciting for kids with a fully customizable timer! With the Pro-Duck-Tivity Timer users can choose from a variety of colors and themes. Kids can enter their name for a personalized message to help keep them motivated and focused on individual tasks.
